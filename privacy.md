# Climb Calendar — Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 9, 2026**

Climb Calendar is a local-only daily planner. This policy is short
because the app is simple: **we do not collect, store, transmit, or
sell any of your data. Ever.**

## What the app stores

Everything you create in Climb Calendar — journal entries and
highlights, routines, tasks, goals, settings, and preferences — is
stored only on your device. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no
analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and makes no network
connections of its own.

## Subscriptions

The Rise subscription is processed entirely by Apple through the App
Store. We never see or handle your payment information. Apple's
handling of your purchase is covered by Apple's own privacy policy.
You can manage or cancel the subscription anytime in your device's
Settings.

## Deleting your data

Because everything lives on your device, deleting the app deletes all
of its data. There is nothing to delete anywhere else.

## Changes to this policy

If the app's behavior ever changes in a way that affects your privacy,
this page will be updated and the effective date above will change.
