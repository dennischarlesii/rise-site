# RISE — Support

RISE is a local-only daily planner. There are no accounts,
no sign-ins, and no data collection: your journal entries, routines,
tasks, and goals live only on your device and never leave it.

If something isn't working — a screen misbehaves, data looks wrong, or
the app crashes — email dennismaherii@gmail.com with a short
description of what you did and what you expected, and I'll take a look.

Deleting your data: everything is stored on-device, so deleting the
app deletes all of its data.
